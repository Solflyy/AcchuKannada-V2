Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 3, 2026

Acchu Kannada ("the App") is developed by Deepak Ram. This privacy policy describes how the App handles user information.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect, use, store, or share any personal information from its users. No user data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

The App may request device permissions (such as access to photos or storage) solely for the purpose of providing its core features — selecting photos for editing and saving edited photos to your gallery. No data accessed through these permissions is collected or shared.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or external SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If the privacy policy changes in the future, users will be notified through an update to this page.

Contact
